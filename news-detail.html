Xinzhou Presents Automated Welding Solutions at the Russia Industrial Exhibition

June 12, 2024ExhibitionRussia

Xinzhou met manufacturers and industry partners in Russia to present intelligent resistance welding equipment and complete production line solutions.

In June 2024, Xinzhou presented its automated resistance welding equipment and complete production line capabilities to manufacturers and industry partners in Russia.

Automation solutions for different production requirements

The exhibition display covered Xinzhou's main equipment categories and turnkey line approach. Visitors were able to review solutions for steel grating, reinforcing mesh, lattice girder and other automated welding applications.

Direct technical communication at the booth

Face-to-face discussions focused on finished product specifications, target production capacity, factory conditions and the level of automation required for each project.

Every production line begins with a clear understanding of the customer's product, capacity target and factory layout.

Support beyond the exhibition

After the exhibition, Xinzhou's sales and engineering teams continue project discussions through solution planning, production line layout, equipment configuration, installation guidance and technical support.
